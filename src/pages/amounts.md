## Amounts & Quantities (EN • DE • IT • GR • BG • SR)

|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

| 🔄 Often | often | oft | spesso | συχνά | често | često |

| ➖ Not enough | not enough | nicht genug | non abbastanza | δεν είναι αρκετά | не е достатъчно | nedovoljno |
